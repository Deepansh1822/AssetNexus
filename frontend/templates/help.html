Getting Started

1

Log In to Your Account

Use your email and password provided by your administrator. Admins have full access; employees see only their assigned assets.

2

Explore the Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a real-time overview of total assets, utilization rates, maintenance status, and total asset value.

3

Add Your First Asset

Navigate to Assets → Add Asset from the sidebar. Fill in the asset details including name, category, serial number, and purchase info.

4

Assign Assets to Employees

Go to Assets → Assign / Return to assign an asset to an employee or mark it as returned.

Assets — Frequently Asked Questions

Go to Assets → Add Asset in the sidebar. Fill in the required fields: Asset Name, Category, and Serial Number. Optional fields include Location, Purchase Date, Cost, and Condition. Click Add Asset to save.

Disposed — The asset has been retired from service.

Maintenance — Frequently Asked Questions

Employees can go to Maintenance → My Requests and click New Request. Select the asset, describe the issue, set the priority, and submit. Admins can also create requests via Maintenance → Create Request.

Pending — Request submitted, awaiting review.
In Progress — Maintenance is actively being performed.
Completed — The issue has been resolved.
Cancelled — The request was cancelled.

Yes. Admins can go to Maintenance → Requests, find the request, and click the edit icon to update its status, add notes, or change the assigned technician.

Reports — Frequently Asked Questions

The Reports page shows the complete asset tracking history — all assignments, returns, and disposals. It includes summary statistics, duration of assignments, and status badges. You can filter by asset/employee name and export the data to CSV.

On the Reports page, click the Export CSV button. The file will include all tracking records with asset name, employee, status, dates, and duration.
